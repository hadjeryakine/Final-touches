Preschool Program

Interactive learning for ages 3–5. Focused on social, motor, and early academic skills.

About the Preschool Program

Our preschool program is designed to nurture curiosity and independence. Through hands-on activities, children learn numbers, letters, shapes, and develop social skills in a fun, engaging environment.

Learning Goals

  • Basic literacy and numeracy
  • Social and emotional development
  • Motor skills and coordination
  • Creative thinking and expression
