Our Services

Autism Support

We offer a dedicated program for children with autism, featuring sensory-friendly activities, one-on-one behavioral therapy, and small class ratios of 1:3 to ensure personalized attention and growth.

Down Syndrome Program

Our Down syndrome program focuses on visual learning, motor skill development, and social engagement through tailored activities, with a 1:4 ratio to support each child’s unique needs.

Speech & Hearing Support

Led by certified speech therapists, this program uses augmentative communication tools and sign language to support children with speech or hearing challenges, fostering confidence in communication.

Inclusive Play Groups

We bring children together through mixed-group activities like art and music, using peer education tools such as storybooks to celebrate differences and promote inclusion in a sensory-friendly environment.

Parent Support

We empower families with monthly workshops on child development, a parent dashboard to track progress (enhanced features for premium users), and community events like Inclusive Play Days.

Flexible Scheduling

Our hours cater to working parents: Monday–Friday 7:00 AM – 6:00 PM, Saturday 8:00 AM – 2:00 PM. Premium users can also access virtual consultations with specialists for added convenience.
